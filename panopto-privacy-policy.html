Privacy Policy for Panopto custom speed

Effective Date: 28/02/2025

1. Information Collection

  • Anonymous Usage Data Only: The extension collects solely anonymous usage data. Specifically, it records only how much time you have saved by using the extension.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or IP address).
  • Opt in / Opt out system: You are able to opt out of any data collection at any time on the main page of the extension.

2. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage Method: All collected data is securely stored in Google Sheets.
  • Access Control: Access to this data is restricted exclusively to an administrator account. No unauthorized third parties have access to these records.
  • Data Protection: We follow industry-standard practices to ensure that your anonymous usage data remains secure.

3. Use of Collected Data

  • Performance Analysis: The anonymous data is used solely to analyze the effectiveness of the extension—specifically, to measure the time saved by users.
  • Improvement of Services: This analysis helps us understand usage patterns and improve the extension’s functionality.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party. Since the data collected is strictly anonymous, there is no risk of personal identification.

5. Data Retention

  • Retention Period: The collected data is retained for as long as necessary to perform the intended analysis and improve the extension. Periodic reviews are conducted to ensure that data is not kept longer than needed.
